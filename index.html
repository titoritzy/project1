Sauti Sol Band

About the Band

Known for its stunning Afropop music, Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Chimano, and Savara Mudigi founded the Kenyan group Sauti Sol in Nairobi in 2005, which has won our hearts with its captivating fusion of Afropop and kapuka music. Their sultry songs, addictive beats, and beautiful vocals have earned them followers worldwide.

Biographies of Team Members

    Bien-Aimé Baraza – Vocalist, guitar, piano

    • Biography: Bien, a member of Kenya's top act group Sauti Sol, was born on December 28, 1987. He is a singer, songwriter, guitarist, and pianist. He graduated in 2011 from the United States International University for Journalism and Media Studies.














    Willis Chimano – Baritone, performer, keytar

    • Nairobi-born Chimano is the third of four children. Despite his introverted childhood, his parents provided the best. His parents were fervent Christians who prayed every night.  He attended Kakamega High School after passing his national examinations. After complaining about his previous school, his parents transferred him to Upper Hill School after two years. He met bandmates Bien Barasa and Savara Mudigi in Upper Hill School.














    Savara Mudigi – Vocalist, record producer, drums, bass guitar

    • Biography: Savara was conceived on May 25, 1987.
    Delvin Mudigi was born and given the name of his parents.
    Savara, one of the band's two members, graduated from Upper Hill High School,
    where they started playing together and soon after met Polycarp Otieno, aka Fancy Fingers.
    Savara enrolled in the Africa Nazarene University to pursue a commerce and finance degree after completing high school.










    Polycarp Otieno – Guitarist, record producer, composer

    • Biography: On November 14, 1987, Polycarp was born in Kenya, where he also spent his childhood. He graduated from Strathmore School and attended high school before enrolling in college. At the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, he continued his education, majoring in Actuarial Science. He made the decision to concentrate only on music with his newly formed band members rather than pursuing further studies.
